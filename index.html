About me

I’ve started my career writing about videogames for some big brazilian publishers and then spent half a decade working at UOL, the largest content, technology and media company in Brazil. In 2014, I left UOL to help launch a startup called Vamo, one of the country’s first integrated payment & ticketing services - an experience that has taught me a whole lot about designing, developing, launching and managing a product slash business. Since then, I’ve also worked on designing and launching other products, mostly for the fintech industry.

In 2020, I’ve decided to join Le Wagon’s Full Stack Web Developer coding bootcamp and shift my career towards being a software developer - so here I am. I love music, travelling, spending time at home with my wife and cats and developing things.

Vamo

Roles: Product Manager, Product Designer, General Manager

Vamo is an integrated payment & ticketing solution launched in 2014 to compete with companies such as Sympla and Eventbrite. Being a part of the launch team, I’m used to wearing many different hats - from product management and design to QA; from running the sales team to doing budgets and market studies. Since mid-2018 I’m responsible for leading our team of 12+ software engineers and account executives at our SP headquarters, answering directly to the founders in the US.

As Vamo's CEO, I’ve been responsible for increasing its gross ticket sales by 88% in 2018 and 101% in 2019, consolidating it as one of the big players in the south region with over 200,000 users, more than 150,000 transactions and 8-digit gross ticket sales.
